Fundamentals of mathematics course that covered linear algebra, multivariable calculus, probability, and optimization. Created weekly lab materials including coding assignments, projects, and additional lectures. Taught weekly lab sections, graded assignments, hosted office hours, and collected feedback. Projects included taking the fundamental mathematics taught in class an translating them into code to create multiple linear regression, knn, and neural networks from scratch. Materials created: https://github.com/eplarocco/3025_Mathematics_for_Data_Science.
